About Us

From the creators of What is That Thing™, Boomlay, Boomlay, Boomlay, Boom!™, and Math Jingle Set A™ 2022.

We present the joint project of 'National Environmental Awareness and Education Act of 2008™'.

Campaigning

For MAPEH (Health), we were tasked by Maam April to campaign 1 of the 3 following topics to lower grade-levels:
- Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002
- National Environmental Awareness and Education Act of 2008
- Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012
