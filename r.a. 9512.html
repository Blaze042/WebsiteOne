R.A. 9512

Section 5 of Republic Act No. 9512, also known as the “National Environmental Awareness and Education Act of 2008”, declared the month of November as National Environmental Awareness Month to promote awareness, especially among the youth, on the importance of protecting the environment and natural resources and the sustainable growth and development of our country. (DENR)

Its Impact To Humans and The Environment?

The impact of the act on the environment and us humans vary greatly where you reside but, the act made most people conscious about the products they buy which in turn, will make people more conscious on what they do to the environment.
